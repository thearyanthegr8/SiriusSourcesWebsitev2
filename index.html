About Us
Sirius Sources Company is a Sourcing Company based in India. We have been the preferred Sourcing Company by many Importers and Retailers in the US, Canada & Europe for over fifteen years now. We offer our Customers, a One-Stop Solution for all their Sourcing needs of Home Textiles, Apparels, Handicrafts, Hard Goods, Leather Goods & Fashion Accessories out of this Region. We undertake Product Design & Development, Factory Sourcing, Production Planning & Management, Quality Inspections, Shipping Coordination & Consolidation on behalf of our customers and are ideally placed to offer the best quality products with timely deliveries. At Sirius we work closely with our clients to ensure their needs, specifications and quality standards to meet the most competitive pricing. We work hard to bring to our Customers the right merchandise with the highest quality standards at the best c1ompetitive prices. In order to achieve this we leverage our in-house resources and close contacts with the factories. We accomplish this because of an uncompromising desire to work in the most efficient manner and with the highest level of communication with both the factories and clients. We recognize that prompt, clear communication is a major priority and make every effort to plan our activities around this requirement.


OUR OBJECTIVE


Is to function as a ‘one stop shop’ for our customers and operate as an extension of their own businesses. We create a close relationship with our customer in order to strongly understand their needs, vision, and potential. Sourcing

Sourcing involves understanding the best manufacturing locations from where to procure various product categories depending on their advantage in terms of such parameters as cost, proximity to market, favoured trading status, etc. and how best the selected suppliers fulfil Customers requirements in terms of volume, quality, social compliance, and delivery. Our understanding of the region’s sourcing capabilities and experienced working relationship with a large network of suppliers is ideal in presenting the knowhow required to draw out significant aspects amid the industry’s current landscape.

Our expertise in sourcing and technical fields for over a decade has helped us gain an in-depth understanding of the products from this region. With our vast experience and access to variety of manufacturing facilities, we ensure that the products are sourced from most reliable companies. All Manufacturing facilities are carefully selected after a detailed study of the processes, equipment, infrastructure, capacities, quality control systems and compliances. Sirius and our associate factories are committed to meet the needs of international trade through highest standards of service.

Quality Standards & Compliances

At Sirius, Quality is of prime importance. Sirius will only work with factories who are socially responsible and comply with the ILO’s international labour standards. By doing so we ensure that the merchandise sold by our clients is free of any contingency that exists with production coming from non-compliant production facilities.

Sirius conducts a multi-phase quality assurance program. Our personnel are stationed at the factories during the production period. We monitor on-line production, mid-production inspections and a review when production is finished. By employing this technique we are able to eliminate last minute rejections and therefore avoid shipping delays. We also work closely with the Testing Laboratories who conform to International Standards laid down by our clients.
